Turning data into wins and wins into fan memories.

Ryan Bernardo currently serves as the Associate Director of Revenue Generation & Data Analytics for the University Athletic Association at the University of Florida. His work focuses on the integration of CRM systems and Marketing Cloud architecture to optimize revenue streams and support the growth of the ticket office.

With a background ranging from business activations with the Washington Commanders to back-end data analytics, he brings a unique perspective that balances technical rigor with a deep understanding of the fan experience.
